Contractors' Collaborative management of Logistics

Collogistics organization provides a capable repository of contractors who besides being state licensed and insured also adhere to high standards of ethics, promote energy conservation, and abide by professional guidelines as members in good standing of Collogistics.

When contractors are members of Collogistics they are able to pass along savings to people who use the contractors' services. Reasons for this are rooted in the principal of "Collaborative management of Logistics". What this translates to is pooled buying power, pooled staging of materials required to render services, properly (and pooled) disposal of material and, to some extent, pooled administration talent based upon Internet technology.
