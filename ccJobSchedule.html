Member Candidate Notice

Qualifying contractors who request our Job Cost Quote Service Module (see Contact to email in your request) can use the Job Cost Module FREE OF CHARGE. Once accepted, you will be given an account to log into and then you can use Job Costing to generate you quotes.

The Collogistics Job Scheduling Module is available to contractors that have used the Job Cost Module after a quotation is accepted by a customer when scheduling is required. The Job Scheduling Module is for member Contractor Collaborators only. Any contractor that uses the free Job Cost Module can apply to become a Contractor Collaborator (using the same email request mechanism). If membership is approved and the nominal membership fee is paid, the Job Scheduling Module is available for usage as long as the license fee is maintained.

Why is the Job Cost Quote Service Module offerred for free while the remainder of modules, such as Job Scheduling, require a license? The simple answer to this question is merely based on typical revenue streams. Most of our Contractor Collaborators offer free estimates. Any time and effort spent conjuring up quotes risks no profit and no return (provided the customer chooses another competing contractor). Our Job Cost service can even be used "on site" with properly equipted laptop or PDAs. We minimize the time and effort required for quotation handling. This, in turn saves you money. Once a customer accepts your quotation you have a new revenue stream and a trivial fraction of that revenue is used to maintain your annual license for the remainder of the Collogistics modules. These modules also tend to pay for themselves in terms of productivity and other benefits.
