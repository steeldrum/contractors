Our Mission

Collogistics provides a capable repository of professionals as well as free or licensed software. We promote the Collaborative Model via our services and software specifically geared for small or medium sized independent contractors.

Contractors who become members are expected to be licensed and abide by our ethical codes and standards. They are deemed "Contractor Collaborators". In addition to the free Jost Cost (a quotation service) we provide a myriad array of modules that, in turn, produce a significant Return On Investment (ROI) for our contractor members.

Part of our role is to educate and orient contractors to show them how to become more competitive and cost-efficient due to inherent benefits accrued via a team approach and collaboration.
